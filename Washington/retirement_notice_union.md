          or
You are giving notice of your resignation (including retirement) and qualify for the Retirement Service Award

Retirement Notice Award (Minimum six months' notice before retirement date)

Qualifications (all staff):

      · Submit this completed Retirement Notification form at least 6 months prior to your planned retirement date
      · Have attained at least age 55 and have at least 12 years of continuous service at retirement since your most recent

            date of hire

Award: If you qualify, you receive a cash award based on this schedule:

     · 12 through 19 years of service: 80 hours of pay, prorated by current FTE.
     · 20 or more years of service: 120 hours of pay prorated by current FTE.

The Retirement Notice Award is based on your regular rate of pay at time of retirement, and is paid on the paycheck prior to
your retirement date with Kaiser Permanente or upon confirmation of your retirement from your manager through the standard
termination notice, whichever is later.

Note: You can only receive the Retirement Notice Award one time. If in the past you were granted this award in cash, or, in
the form of additional PTO under the legacy Notice Award program, you're ineligible to receive the award a second time.

Retirement Service Award

When you retire, you get a Retirement Service Award (cash payment) if you qualify under terms of your collective
bargaining agreement.

While you are not required to provide advance notice in addition to meeting the qualifications to qualify for the
Retirement Service Award, please complete and submit the form at least four weeks in advance of your last day
of employment to the National HR Service Center Fax: (877) 477-2329.

If you qualify, the Retirement Service Award is paid on the paycheck prior to your retirement (separation) date with Kaiser
Permanente or upon confirmation of your retirement from your manager through the standard termination notice, whichever is
later.

Questions? Call the National HR Service Center at (877) 457-4772.
